About Me

assignment CV File open_in_new LinkedIn

I'm currently working at Deloitte as a Tax Consultant, supporting multinational clients with financial analysis and structured reporting. I handle financial data, build detailed tables for senior executives, and help turn complex numbers into clear insights.

Alongside work, I'm preparing for the CFA Level I exam, with the goal of moving into portfolio and asset management.

Before Deloitte, I worked in B2B sales at Tide Platform and completed an internship at HP Inc., supporting enterprise clients. I hold a Master's degree in International Business from the University of National and World Economy. My thesis focused on Amazon's global expansion and received the highest grade in my class.

Outside of work, I competed as a national-level wrestler, representing Bulgaria. That experience shaped my discipline and how I handle pressure.

I'm interested in history and leadership. I believe that understanding the past helps make sense of the future, especially when it comes to how people think, decide, and lead under uncertainty.

Based in Sofia. Fluent in English and Bulgarian.
